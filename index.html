Why Choose FORGE

NOT JUST A GYM.
A PLACE WHERE CHANGE HAPPENS.

Thousands of Melbourne members have made FORGE their training home. Here's what makes us different — and why it matters for your results.

01

24/7 Access — Because Life Doesn't Work 9 to 5

Elite members get full 24/7 keycard access to the entire facility. Whether you train at 5am before work or 11pm after your shift — FORGE is open. No more gym hours limiting your routine.

02

Coaches Who Actually Coach — Not Just Watch

Our trainers are on the floor during every class — correcting form, scaling exercises, pushing you harder. We cap group classes at 14 people maximum so nobody gets lost in the crowd. This isn't a class you survive; it's one that transforms you.

03

$800,000 in Commercial-Grade Equipment

Eleiko barbells and plates, Life Fitness cardio machines, a dedicated functional rig, 28 Keiser indoor bikes, heavy bags, and a full recovery zone with infrared sauna and compression boots. Broken equipment gets repaired same-day — always.

04

Nutrition Guidance Included — Not an Upsell

All Elite and Performance members receive access to our in-house nutrition coach for a monthly check-in. We believe training without nutrition guidance is like driving with one hand on the wheel. Our approach is practical and sustainable — no crash diets.

05

Progress Tracking That Keeps You Accountable

Every member gets a FORGE profile tracking strength benchmarks, body composition measurements via InBody scan, class attendance, and programme milestones. Your data is visible on the app so you can see real month-over-month progress.

06

A Community That Actually Shows Up

Our member community is genuinely supportive — not performative. Members know each other by name, celebrate milestones, and hold each other accountable. We run quarterly challenges, social events, and charity fitness drives.

07

No Lock-In. No Bull. Cancel Anytime.

We don't trap you in 12-month contracts. No joining fees. No high-pressure sales. We believe if we deliver results, you'll stay. Our average member retention rate is 14 months — well above the industry average of 5.

08

7-Day Free Trial — Judge Us Before You Commit

New members get 7 days of unlimited access completely free. Train every day that week. Attend every class. Then decide. No credit card required to start. We're confident enough in what we've built to let the experience do the talking.

Our Programs

EVERY PROGRAM.
ONE PURPOSE.

Whether you're losing your first kilogram or chasing a powerlifting PB — our programmes deliver measurable outcomes, not just something to do.

Program 01

Strength & Powerlifting

Our structured strength programme uses periodised training — linear, wave, and block — to progressively build your squat, bench, and deadlift. Suitable for beginners and competitive powerlifters alike.

  • Technique assessment in first session
  • Percentage-based loading personalised to you
  • Peaking programmes for competitions
  • Small group coaching — max 8 per session
Included — Elite & Performance Plans
Program 02

HIIT & Metabolic Conditioning

30-minute high-intensity sessions built around AMRAP, EMOM, and Tabata formats. Designed to maximise caloric burn, improve VO2 max, and build a base of functional fitness for everyday life.

  • Heart rate zone training with monitors available
  • Varied formats — never the same session twice
  • Scaled options for all fitness levels
  • Post-session breakdown via FORGE app
From $39/month — Starter Plan
Program 03

1-on-1 Personal Training

Completely individualised sessions with one of our certified coaches. Whether you have specific goals, injuries to work around, or simply want faster results — PT delivers precision that group classes can't.

  • Initial movement screen & goal-setting session
  • Custom programming between sessions
  • Nutrition integration and tracking support
  • Weekly check-ins via the FORGE app
From $85/session · Packs available
Program 04

Boxing & Combat Fitness

Technical boxing combined with serious conditioning. Learn real punching technique, footwork, and combinations while burning up to 700 calories per session. No sparring required — ever.

  • Beginner-friendly — gloves provided
  • Heavy bag circuits and pad work
  • Footwork and defensive drills
  • 12-round structure like real bouts
Included — Elite & Performance Plans
Program 05

Yoga & Mobility

Recovery is training. Our yoga and active mobility sessions are designed specifically for athletes and gym-goers — preventing injury, reducing soreness, and improving range of motion that makes every other session better.

  • Sports-specific mobility protocols
  • Guided breathwork and recovery techniques
  • Injury prevention focus — not just stretching
  • Morning and evening sessions available
Included — All Plans
